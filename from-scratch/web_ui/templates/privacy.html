Data & Privacy

NeuralAI is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how your data is handled:

Chat Conversations

Your conversations are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for session persistence. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

Uploaded Files

Files you upload for RAG (PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD) are processed on the server and stored temporarily. You can delete them at any time from the Files tab. File contents are used only to answer your questions.

Neural Uplink

When complex tasks are routed to the agent network, only the relevant prompt and context are sent to the uplink servers. No personal data or files are shared.

Model

NeuralAI runs a local model (SmolLM2-360M) on the server. Inference happens on CPU, and no data is sent to external AI providers during normal chat.

Your Control

You can clear your conversation history at any time by clicking "New chat" or clearing your browser data. Uploaded files can be deleted individually from the Files tab.
